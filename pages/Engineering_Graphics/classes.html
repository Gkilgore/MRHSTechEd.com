Engineering Graphics I

In this class, students will learn the fundamentals of technical drawing, computer-aided design (CAD), and 3D modeling. Through engaging projects, they will develop problem-solving skills and gain a strong foundation in engineering and design principles. This course is perfect for students interested in engineering, architecture, or any STEM-related field. Get ready to bring your ideas to life!

Honors Engineering Graphics - Level II

Building on the fundamentals from Engineering Graphics, this class dives deeper into 3D modeling, engineering design processes, and real-world problem-solving. Students will work on more complex projects, preparing them for higher-level engineering courses and future STEM careers. This course is ideal for those passionate about innovation, design, and technology.
