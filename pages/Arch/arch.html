Marlboro High School Architecture

Architecture 1

About Architecture 1

Architecture I is an introductory course focused on the principles of architectural design, construction techniques, and computer-assisted design (CAD). Students create residential plans and build models to understand real-world applications.

Course Work
  • Introduction to Architecture
  • CAD Design
  • Structural Systems
  • Construction Techniques

Architecture 2

About Architecture 2

Architecture II builds upon the fundamentals of Architecture I, focusing on advanced CAD techniques, commercial design, and sustainability. Students create detailed commercial and residential plans, along with physical and digital models.

Course Work
  • Advanced CAD Design
  • Commercial Architecture
  • Sustainable Design
  • Landscape Planning

Architecture 3

About Architecture 3

Architecture III is an advanced honors course that immerses students in professional-level architectural design. Students work on complex real-world projects, engage in urban planning, and develop a comprehensive portfolio for college or career pursuits.
